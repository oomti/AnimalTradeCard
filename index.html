The White Owl

The white owl symbols wisdom, guidance in darkness, and connection to mystery

  • Scientific name : Tyto alba
  • Habitat: Farmland, grassland
  • Length: 33-49 cm
  • Wingspan: 80-95 cm

According to Wikipedia Barn owl is the most widespread bird in the world. It`s a predator hunting during the night time using it`s acute sense of hearing. The white owl can make an acoustic mindmap of it`s sorrounding, and can hunt without a trace of light.
